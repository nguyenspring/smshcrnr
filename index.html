Information

Smash Corner is a biweeky Super Smash Bros. Melee tournament series hosted by Alex Cottrell, Spring Nguyen, and Nicolas Rivera.
Time: Wednesday nights at 5:30 PM
Schedule:
5:30 - Registration begins
6:00 - Doubles bracket starts
7:30 - Singles bracket starts
11:00 - Venue Closes
Location:
Clarkson's Corner
4 Grayrock Road, Scarsdale, NY, 10583
Price:
$5 Venue fee(Comes with free drink and snack)
$5 Singles entry
$5 Doubles entry per person
Payouts:
1st place will receive 60% of the pot
2nd place will receive 30% of the pot
3rd place will receieve 10% of the pot
